Using Kamkrle without an account

Without an account, your data is saved only in this browser, on this device. If you clear your browser data, switch browsers, or use another device, this progress will not be there and cannot be recovered.

You can add an email at any time later to back up and sync everything you've tracked so far — nothing you do as a guest is wasted if you decide to do that.

Quick Tips

  • Daily Routine — check off today's tasks here. Scroll down for a 30-day heatmap of your consistency.
  • Calendar Workspace — browse past months, see each day's completion %, and view a 30-day trend chart.
  • Manage Routine — add up to 10 recurring tasks. Changes apply to today's checklist right away.
  • Streaks — a "perfect day" means 100% of that day's tasks are checked off. Your streak counts consecutive perfect days.
  • Sync & Theme — sign in to sync your routine across devices, and use the moon icon anytime to switch between day and night mode.
